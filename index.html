Coke Cola Cosmo Shift Logo Design

Coke Cola Cosmo Shift Logo Design

Brief
Coca-Cola Beverages Africa (Kenya) office was moving location. They were moving to a bigger office to accommodate their staff. The brief was to create a campaign to excite and introduce the whole staff about this office shift.

Creative Solution
We created a logo called Cosmo and included all the prominent brand colours of all Coca-Cola drinks. For eg. Coca-Cola Red, Schweppes Yellow, Powerade Blue, Sprite Green, Fanta Orange.

Cosmo is derived from the word Cosmos. A continuous area of limitless expanse which is free and available.

Cosmo will support people’s growth, give space for ideas to be shared, a space where people will come together to achieve common goals.

Cosmo will be a safe space that promotes a continuous flow of energy with a collaborative spirit; a place that powers limitless growth, allowing you to expand into your potential and ultimately reach greater heights than ever imagined. Cosmo will support coming together to achieve common goals.

The Cosmo campaign panned out in different phases and activities. From a teaser email campaign, countdown posters, moving day activities, office branding suggestions, and a welcome party at the new office location.

Garden 6 Brand Identity Design

Garden 6 Brand Identity Design

Brief

To design a logo for a new 22-storey residential tower, emerging among Parsi heritage structures, featuring 15 exclusive 4BHK apartments, each occupying a single floor with a 2000 sq. ft. carpet area. Priced at Rs. 8-10 Crores, the design focuses on eco-friendly luxury for affluent Parsis.

Creative Solution

Garden 6’s logo encapsulates the essence of its luxurious homes and rich flora. Drawing inspiration from Arabian floral motifs and calligraphy, the design merges the letter ‘G’ from “Garden,” with elegant leaves, flowers, and the numeral ‘6.’ These elements are artistically integrated into a vertical emblem that reflects sophistication and a homage to nature’s beauty. The final logo is a testament to the residence’s unique charm and its commitment to blending modern luxury with natural elegance.

Rigman Rebrand Design

Rigman Rebrand Design

Brief

Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) supplier and maker Rigman is situated in the UK and serves the oil rig, construction, and marine industries. It's a leading provider of PPE in the Middle East & Europe. They desired a more contemporary rebranding. However, they were unwilling to abandon the red color, shield, and wings that make up their current logo.

Creative Solution

The innovative solution was to give each component a more solid, modern appearance. To make it stand out more, switch the typeface from a serif to a thicker one. We treated the wings the same way. The end product is a stronger logo that identifies a producer of personal protective equipment.

Additionally, we created their e-commerce website. The designs were centered on making the products look amazing by showcasing them in their natural habitat in a larger-than-life manner. The products were photographed separately in a studio and then composited into the appropriate settings.

VBL Brand Identity Design

VBL Brand Identity Design

Concept

VBL is an exclusive and exotic eco sanctuary located on Vipingo Beach, north coast of Mombasa. With its pristine white sandy beaches, elegant villas and extraordinary marine life, VBL is a private and unique paradise.

We developed every aspect of the brand identity, from strategic brand positioning to name generation of locations and structures for the VBL property. Inspired by the unique biodiversity of the north coast and Vipingo, we developed a logotype that reflects the repetitive patterns found in nature.

When closely observed the bluer lagoons, the gentle crash of tide on the white sandy beaches and rare botany creates a unique pattern of undulating waves, this pattern is what inspires this visual identity.

Every touchpoint - brochures, signage, stationery, toiletries, uniforms, website design and Communication - is designed to echo the rarity and splendour of VBL
