At Edgi Studio, we marry science's precision with art's fluidity, revolutionizing branding and advertising from the UAE's vibrant core. Founders Emily White and Arun Balagopal—her British design elegance intertwined with his award-winning, global creative direction—fuel Edgi with innovation, creativity, and two decades of expertise. We're more than an agency; we're a cradle of groundbreaking solutions and unmatched artistry.

Edgi Studio's edge lies in our innovative model—collaborating with industry experts to craft solutions that surpass client expectations, affordably. This fusion offers multinational agency quality with the agility and cost-efficiency of a startup, focusing not just on services, but on creating enduring impacts.

At Edgi Studio, excellence is paramount; each project benefits from Emily and Arun's rigorous oversight, ensuring top-quality outcomes. Opting for Edgi means embracing cost-effective, quality-driven solutions and innovative problem-solving. Welcome to the future of branding, where your vision becomes our mission, and your success, our ultimate goal. Here, design is both an art and a science.
